About CodeVille




One of the main reason over 80% of engineering graduates are unemployable is because the curriculum that they are taught at colleges and universities are mostly outdated, students also do not build many projects leading to them having inferior pratical knowledge.

CodeVille is a platform designed to address these challenges and to enable students to gain useful programming skills.

Designed exclusively for Bangaloreans, it includes hands-on modules for web and mobile development.

Students get access to online(in development) and offline resources with individual mentorship to guide and help them overcome any hard obstacles that they face while learning.
